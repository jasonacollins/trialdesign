In this module, you will learn how to make important decisions about your trial, such as choosing a sample size. We will also discuss how to analyse the data you collected to conclude whether your treatment induced a change.

By the end of this module, you will be able to answer the following questions:

  1. What types of errors can result in experimental data analysis?
  2. How can we conduct a power analysis to decide on the sample size for a trial?
  3. How can we interpret effect sizes?
